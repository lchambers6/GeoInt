A little About Luke

Luke Chambers is a dedicated environmental professional with a strong background in environmental engineering and software development. He holds a Master of Science in Earth and Atmospheric Science and a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology. His passion for environmentalism began early, leading him to work as a Sustainability Consultant with Waste Management before transitioning into data analysis and software engineering.

Although he spent several years as a software engineer—designing cloud infrastructure and leading development teams—Luke remains eager to return to his true passion: environmental sustainability. His diverse experience in data analysis, waste management consulting, and sustainability initiatives uniquely positions him to contribute meaningfully to sustainable business practices and environmental engineering. With expertise in data management, business intelligence dashboards, and software engineering best practices, Luke is committed to leveraging technology to drive impactful environmental solutions.
